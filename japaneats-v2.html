    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why do you charge upfront with no refunds? ▼
    We've had customers book multiple restaurants and not show up, damaging our relationships with these establishments. The upfront payment ensures only serious diners use our service. This policy protects both the restaurants and our ability to continue helping travelers.
    What if I need to cancel my reservation? ▼
    Once confirmed with the restaurant, bookings cannot be cancelled and payments are non-refundable. Please be 100% certain before booking. If you're unsure about your plans, wait until you're certain before using our service.
    What if the restaurant is fully booked? ▼
    This is the ONLY case where we provide a full refund. If we cannot secure your booking after trying multiple times, we'll refund your $20 immediately and suggest alternative restaurants.
    How quickly will I get confirmation? ▼
    Most bookings are confirmed within 24 hours during Japanese business hours (9AM-9PM JST). We'll email you immediately once confirmed with all details including the restaurant's confirmation number.
    Which restaurants can you book? ▼
    We can book any restaurant that accepts phone reservations. However, we cannot book: ramen shops (no reservations), fast food chains, some exclusive establishments that prohibit third-party bookings (like Sukiyabashi Jiro), and restaurants requiring membership.
    Do you handle special requests? ▼
    Yes! We communicate all dietary restrictions, allergies, celebrations, seating preferences, and any other special requests in Japanese to ensure the restaurant is fully prepared for your visit.
